What We're Doing

Just trying to help

We are using 3D printers to print the materials for make-shift face shields to donate to local healthcare providers. The parts are made by 3D printing the frame with PETG or PLA printer filament and modifying Apollo transparency film with our C02 laser. We've been using a version created by 3DVerkstan and have refined a model and process that allows us to print as many as we can at a time.

Want to help?

  • I have a 3D printer.
  • I have a CNC or laser.
  • I DO NOT have a 3D printer.

No printer? No problem.
